Problem / Opportunity

Detours create uncertainty when riders need precision most.

Pain Points

Riders face fragmented alerts, unclear alternatives, and inconsistent timing confidence during disruptions.

Stakeholders

Daily commuters, occasional riders, transit agencies, and support teams all rely on coherent detour communication.

Contextual Factors

City density, accessibility needs, weather, and multi-modal transfer pressures shape detour decision quality.

Our Solution

A detour-first Transit redesign focused on actionable confidence.

We introduce dynamic wayfinding, confidence scoring, and mode-aware alternatives to reduce decision friction in disrupted trips.
